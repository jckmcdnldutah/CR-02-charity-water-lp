Our Mission

Charity Water is a non-profit organization bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. We use 100% of public donations to fund water projects, and we prove every project with photos and GPS on our public profile.

Our Projects

Charity Water works on a variety of water projects including drilled wells, protected springs, and Biosand filters to provide clean water to communities around the world.

Our Blog

Read our blog to get the latest updates on our work, inspiring stories from the field, and to learn more about the global water crisis.
